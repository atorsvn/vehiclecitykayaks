Frequently Asked Questions

You can contact us via email at vehiclecitykayaks@gmail.com for a quick response, or you can call us at (810) 513-2439 We are also available to answer questions via our social media pages.

We are located at Thread Lake, just a few miles from downtown Flint. Our launch site is behind the Community Building at McKinley Park. Look for our signs!

Our hours vary by season and weather. Please check our online booking system for the most up-to-date availability and operating hours.

We offer single kayaks and tandem kayaks (for two people). All rentals are perfect for exploring Thread Lake.

We offer 2-hour rentals. A single kayak is $25, and a tandem kayak is $40.

Yes, all kayak rentals include paddles and life jackets. Pedal boat rentals include life jackets. We will also provide basic instructions for your safety and enjoyment.

We recommend bringing water, sunscreen, a hat, and a pair of sunglasses. We also suggest wearing comfortable clothing that you don't mind getting wet.

While we do accept walk-ins, we highly recommend booking online in advance, especially on weekends, to ensure a watercraft is available for you.

We offer a full refund for cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance. For cancellations within 24 hours, we can provide a credit for a future rental.

For weather-related cancellations (e.g., thunderstorms or high winds), we will contact you to reschedule your rental or issue a full refund. Your safety is our top priority.

Yes, all rentals must be used within the current season. Our season typically runs from late spring to early fall. You can find exact dates on our website.

There is no limit! Feel free to purchase as many rentals as you and your friends or family need for a great day on the water.
